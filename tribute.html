About Albert Einstein

Albert Einstein was born on March 14, 1879, in Ulm, Germany. He is best known for his theory of relativity, which revolutionized our understanding of space, time, and gravity.

His contributions to physics earned him the Nobel Prize in Physics in 1921 for his explanation of the photoelectric effect. Einstein's work laid the foundation for modern physics and had a profound impact on science and technology.
